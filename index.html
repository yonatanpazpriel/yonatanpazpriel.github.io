About Me

I'm a sophomore at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. I'm particularly interested in Natural Language Processing and its applications in analyzing public discourse and policy. Throughout my time at Berkeley, I've worked with professors and government agencies to apply NLP to real-world problems.

While at Berkeley, I've also founded the Cal Interfaith Association and compete on the Cal Mock Trial team. I maintain a collection of carnivorous plants and am a huge David Bowie fan.
